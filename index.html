Who we are ?

At French Alps Adventures, we are passionate explorers dedicated to sharing the natural beauty and hidden gems of the French Alps. Founded by Guillaume Bringazen with the help of experienced mountaineers and local guides, our mission is to craft memorable journeys that connect adventurers with breathtaking landscapes and local cultures. With a focus on safety, sustainability, and personalized experiences, we aim to provide not just a hike, but a gateway to the soul of the mountains with the possibility to make the region easier for the locals producers with your help. Join us and experience the Alps as never before, guided by a team who truly knows the trails, peaks, and people that make this region extraordinary !
